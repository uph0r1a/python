The rain had just stopped when the city lights flickered back to life, reflecting off the wet pavement like scattered stars. A quiet hum drifted from distant traffic while the smell of damp concrete lingered in the air. Somewhere above, a window was left open, and music spilled out in uneven fragments, mixing with the soft footsteps of people heading home. For a brief moment, everything felt paused, as if the night itself was taking a slow breath before moving on again.
